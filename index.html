Aaron Rodgers Is Back, Baby!

The reigning NFL MVP shows up to training camp in style

After months of holding out on contract negotiations, the famed quarterback returns from sunny California looking as relaxed as ever. Whatever beef he has had with Packers front office looks to be squashed and the man is ready to play ball. The three-time MVP seeks his second Lombardi Trophy to add to his legacy.


Rodgers pulled up to Packers facility rocking some killer Heatwave sunglasses and a shirt featuring Kevin, from NBC sitcom "The Office".


Aaron Rodger's guide to look like it's first day of class at university:

  • Meme shirt
  • Backpack
  • Garrish shades
  • Manbun

Look at that swagger!
